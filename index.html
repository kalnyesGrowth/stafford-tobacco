Who We Are

More Than a Shop. A Community.

Stafford Tobacco is where neighbors become friends and first-time visitors leave feeling like family. We are a proud Black-owned business with two locations in Stafford, offering a carefully curated selection of premium cigars, vapes, hookah, and accessories at prices that respect your wallet.

Whether you are browsing our walk-in humidor for the perfect smoke or settling into our lounge for an afternoon cigar, you will find a staff that genuinely cares about your experience, not just the sale.
